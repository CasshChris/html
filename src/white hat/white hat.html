White Hat Hacking
 

White Hat Hacking is a field that is needed more and more each day.
White Hat Hackers or Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), are hackers that
are hired or contracted by many different companies to secure
their services, software, hardware, and websites from Black Hat hackers.
Black hat hackers are people that hack others without their permission to
do so. The more that the world moves to online usage as opposed to manual usage,
having digital lifestyles can cause society to think more about our
cybersecurity, it is something that can be detrimental to our way of being.
With a little bit of access to your information, computer or wifi networks any
“Black Hat Hacker” can get into your emails, access bank accounts,
lock you out of your wifi network, make configurations to your computer,
and steal your identity.There was a study done in 2007 by the University of
Maryland that stated that

there is a cyber attack every 39 seconds on the web.
In recent years, companies like Facebook, Google, Instagram,
and Snapchat have been getting hacked more and more often.
That is where white hat hackers or ethical hackers come in.

There are organizations that give certifications for White Hat Hackers such as the EC-Council.
To become a certified White Hat hacker all you would need is to be good with command-line interfaces,
and the EC-Council recommends that you have a basic understanding of what Windows and Linux are.
